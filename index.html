About Professor Ahmed Haleem

Professor Ahmed Haleem is a distinguished academic, consultant, and entrepreneur with more than 25 years of expertise in architecture, sustainable design, and urban planning.

As the Founder & CEO of Haleem Consult Group (HCG), he has directed and advised on major national projects in collaboration with leading institutions, including the Grand Imam of Al-Azhar, the Ministry of Youth and Sports, the Egyptian Armed Forces Engineering Authority, the National Construction Company, Arab International Optronics, and DASCO.

Alongside his consulting practice, Professor Haleem serves as an Associate Professor of Architecture at Helwan University, where he is recognized for his commitment to education, research, and mentoring the next generation of architects. His career reflects a unique balance between academic excellence and impactful consulting leadership, making him a respected figure both nationally and internationally.

Professional Journey

Professor Ahmed Haleem's professional journey spans over 25 years, marked by a continuous dedication to architecture, education, and consulting at the highest levels.

He began his academic path at Helwan University, where he earned his Bachelor of Architecture (1999), followed by a Master's in Architectural Engineering (2004), and later completed his PhD in Architecture (2010). This strong foundation established him as a scholar committed to both theoretical research and practical design solutions.

Soon after completing his studies, he joined Helwan University's Faculty of Engineering as a lecturer, steadily progressing to his current role as Associate Professor of Architecture. Over the years, he also contributed as an Associate Professor of Architecture (Part-time) at AASTMT, where he delivered specialized courses in sustainable design, urban planning, and heritage conservation. His dual focus on academic teaching and research has enabled him to mentor countless students and contribute to scholarly publications and conferences.

Parallel to his academic career, Professor Haleem expanded into architectural consultancy, driven by a passion to link academic knowledge with real-world impact. In 2014, he founded Haleem Consult Group (HCG), a firm specializing in urban planning, sustainable design, and engineering advisory. Under his leadership, HCG became a trusted partner to some of Egypt's most prestigious organizations, including the Grand Imam of Al-Azhar, the Ministry of Youth and Sports, the Egyptian Armed Forces Engineering Authority, the National Construction Company, Arab International Optronics, and DASCO.

In recent years, Professor Haleem has also assumed an international leadership role as the Director of the Arab China Commercial Association and Cultural Exchange Council, where he is actively promoting Arabâ€“Chinese cooperation in trade, education, architecture, and cultural exchange. This position reflects his broader vision of fostering cross-cultural collaboration and sustainable international partnerships.

Today, his journey represents a rare balance of academic excellence, consulting expertise, and international influence. From classrooms and lecture halls to high-profile national projects and global cultural initiatives, Professor Haleem continues to make a profound impact on both the academic community and the architectural profession.
